Proactive Business Alerts for RFPs & Keyword Matching

Pain Point Covered: Alert for keyword RFPs, purchase trends, automation, daily work validation

Workflow:

Trigger: New email/RFP uploaded to Gmail/Google Drive.

Gemini: Scan for specific keywords (e.g., “RFP”, “credit”, “enquiry”, “validation”).

Google Sheets: Log matching items and send instant alert (email/Slack/WhatsApp).
